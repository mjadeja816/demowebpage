About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering at MNIT Jaipur. I received my PhD from DAIICT in Theoretical Computer Science.

My work sits at the intersection of graph based learning, network science, and generative AI systems for education and institutional productivity.

Core strengths

  • Teaching and curriculum design for Python, discrete math, data structures, DBMS, and social network analysis
  • Research on graphs, learning on networks, and applied AI in real world systems
  • Workshops for educators and professionals on AI literacy, prompt design, and automation

Research and professional focus

Graph learning

Graph Neural Networks, representation learning, and graph based reasoning for complex relational data.

Theoretical foundations

Algorithmic thinking and formal reasoning, with a focus on clear structure and correctness.

AI assisted learning design, custom assistants, and scalable workflows for teaching and assessment.
